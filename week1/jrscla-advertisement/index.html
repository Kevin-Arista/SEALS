What is Jr.SCLA?

SCLA is An immersive 10-week Summer experience where young men of color develop as technology leaders. Middle school students will experience first hand learning, receive mentoring and instruction to develop Leadership Skills, Computer Science Skills, Design Thinking Skills, and Entrepreneurial/Project Management Skills. This program will be very beneficial to develop your character not only to learn expand your knowledge on coding but to expand your career interest! TxT is truly benificial because it provides you the chance for you to learn coding for free! You also get to meet new people when working in teams and get to network - like what professionals do!

Experience

Hello my name is Kevin Arista and I was a 10th grader when I joined SCLA for the summer of 2020. I met other young males of color and we learned the basics of coding through challenges to prepare us for our main goal - to build an application that would help our community. Can you imagine, saying that when you were in middle school you developed an app that helped your community?! As the software developer in my team, I developed the code of our app called Street Hub using MEANStack which is a set of coding languages used to develop an app. This experience taught me certain tools that professionals at Google, Snapchat, and Instagram use! I also learned and had fun working with my teammates.
